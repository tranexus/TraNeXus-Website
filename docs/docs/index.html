TraNexus Approaches Travel Differently

Travel is an area that’s changed little over the centuries. Today we travel by plane and train instead of horse and sailing ships, but we’re still carrying paper-based documents, multiple currencies, maps and more. There’s a better way.

TraNexus introduces a series of blockchain-based apps, technologies and more that streamline travel. We’re bringing the fun and adventure back into travel for the new generation of travelers. Our project focuses on apps that manage documents and currencies, experiences to enhance the fun, and melding travel with technology to transform the industry. We streamline the travel process for business travelers and tourists alike while creating an entirely new travel experience for green-minded adventure-seekers around the world.

Simplified Travel

TraNexus Wallet

The TraNexus Wallet simplifies your travel by storing important travel documents, monetary assets, cryptocurrency (BTC, WAVES, and ETH) and other travel items in one convenient, portable, and secure place.

Better Travel

TraNexus Travel Assistant

A digital travel companion providing constant support–from lists of routes, hotels, and restaurants based on user input, to storing your itinerary in a single location for easy reference—TraNexus Travel Assistant also aids users in locating vendors who accept cryptocurrency as a form of payment anywhere in the world. Search and customize your list of places to go, things to see, and restaurants to explore.

Shared Travel

TraNexus Explorer

Transforming “blockchain explorers” into true global adventures, TraNexus Explorer changes your geographical location into a 4D journal that allows travelers to experience—via blockchain-based text, audio, image, and video messages—historical events and past user visits to that location. In addition, Explorer offers users the opportunity to physically “Quest” (generate smart contracts) for a variety of incentivizing token rewards, turning travel into the ultimate interactive augmented reality game.
